This is a coxinha recipe guide

  • 1 and 1/2 of curd cheese
  • 1 soup spoon of any sauce
  • 1/2 grated onion
  • 2 soup spoons of sugar
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 3 cubes of chicken broth
  • 2 soup spoons of margarine
  • 4 cups of wheat flour

  1. The curd cheese makes it more soft
  2. A spoon of sauce and grated onion add more taste
  3. Add a little bit of sugar to break the extra salt
  4. Milk is needed to mix all ingredients
  5. Chicken broth to raise the chicken taste
  6. Margarine is optional
  7. Wheat flour is the base of all the recipe

Important note: Please follow all the step-by-step in order
